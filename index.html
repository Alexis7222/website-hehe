About Me

My Spiritual Journey

For over 15 years, I've walked the path of spiritual discovery and personal transformation. My journey began during a profound period of reflection following a major life transition, which led me to explore ancient wisdom traditions from across the world.

After studying with respected teachers in meditation and spiritual direction spaces, I developed a unique approach that honors traditional wisdom while embracing modern understanding of psychology and personal growth.

My Philosophy

I believe that each person carries within them a wellspring of wisdom and potential waiting to be nourished. True spiritual growth isn't about following someone else's path, it's about discovering your own authentic connection to something greater.

My coaching approach is grounded in the principle that transformation happens when we align our daily actions with our deepest values and spiritual insights. Through compassionate guidance and practical tools, I help you bridge the gap between spiritual awareness and everyday living.
